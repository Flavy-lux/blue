    About Us

    We are the foremost Unga & Animal Feeds Company in Kenya

    Afyagross Enterprises was established and registered under Act (Cap 499) laws of Kenya on twenty ninth day of July the year 2013 as a partnership company under certificate number BN/2013/229196.The company was registered wit two directors who are actively involved in the day to day running of the company.

    Afyagross Enterprises has a sister company namely;Green Field Millers Finest Ltd which was incorporated under Act,2015 on 14th May 2018 as a private Limited Company.

    The Company is divided into four divisions namely,Finance & administration, Sales & marketing,procurement & stores and Production & Quality Assuarance all headed by the two directors.

    Afyagross business is conducted by its employees,managers and officers,under the direction of the chief executive officer (the CEO) and the oversight of the Board, to enhance the long-term value of the Company for its shareholders.The Board of Directors monitors the performance of the CEO and senior management to ensure thet the long-term interests of the shareholders are being served.

    As a professional Feed Company,we offer specialized services in manufacturing and distribution of all categories of Animal Feeds, additives and mineral supplements to the kenya farming community.These products are of high quality,delivered on time,at a competitive pricing,supported by leading edge technical advice and service to the livestock nutrition industry.

    PRODUCTS

    We offer a wide range of products including cattle feeds,poulrty feeds and pig feeds

    POULTRY FEED

    Chick mash:Feed to young laying or breeding chicken from 6 to 8 weeks of age as a sole source of nutrients.Feed dry up to a total of 2 kilograms of feed per chick during this period.
    Growers mash:Feed for laying or breeding chicken from 6 weeks up to the point of lay.On average one pullet will consume 7 kilograms of feed during this period.
    Layers mash:Feed to laying hens producing eggs.Feed at a rate of 140 grams per hen per day.
    Fast-grow broiler starter mash:For use as a sole source of nutrients for chicken specifically bred for meat from zero to 3 weeks of age.The consumption as 0.9 to 1 Kilograms of feed over this period.
    Fast-grow broiler finisher mash:For use as a sole source of nutrients for chicken specifically bred for meat from 4 to 6 weeks of age.They consume 2.8 to 3 Kilograms during this period on average.
    Kienyeji mash/Kienyeji layer mash:For use as a sole source of nutrients for free range chicken from 4 weeks of age.

    PIG FEED

    Sow and wearner meal:For use as a sole source of nutrients for growing pigs up to 55 kilograms live weight and adult breeding pigs.On average consume 3kg per day except suckling sow that consume 4.5 to 5.5 Kilograms of feed per day depending on litter size.
    Pig finishing meal:For use as a sole source of nutrient for pig weighing over 55 kilograms live weight.Over this period,each pig consumes 2.6 kilograms of finishing meal per day.

    Cattle FEEDs

    Extra yield dairy meal:For use as supplement for feeding standard dairy cows yielding from 10 to 15 litres of milk per day.Feed 1 kilogram of feed for every 2 kilograms of milk produced above 7 litres of milk per day.
    Standard dairy meal:For use as a supplement for feeding high milk yielding dairy cattle from 15 to 20 litres of milk per day.Feed 1 kg for every 2.5kg of milk produced above 10 litres per day.
    Dairy meal with Yeast Culture:For use as supplement feeding high milk yielding dairy cattle producing more than 20 litres of milk per day.Feed 1 kg for every 2.5kg of milk produced above 10 litres per day.
    Dairy maziwa lick:Mineral supplements for optimum milk Production
    Dry cow dairy lick:A mineral supplement for dry cow
